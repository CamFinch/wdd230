White Space and Clean Design

Apple

apple.com

Apple is a great example of White Space and Clean Design as they are known for their simplicity. In the screenshot above you are able to clearly read all information and distunguish sepeartation. There is no clutter to be found.

Rule of Thirds

Chick-Fil-A

chick-fil-a.com

Chick-Fil-A's website is a good example for the rule of thirds as they align all the important and eye catching elemnts in thirds. They are not close together and are evenly spread. If you were to use a grid the image of the sandwhich and the order buttons all fall on the 4 points.

Repetition

Lowes

lowes.com

Lowes does a good job showing repetion in the screenshot above as not only the spacing has stayed even throughout, they repeat the color coordination in all for elements shown. They split each title into a top and bottom and kept the two shades of blue the same for each top and bottom on all 4. THe size of the logos on each side are also the same and are also the same color.
